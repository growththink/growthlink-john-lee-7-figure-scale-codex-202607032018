Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is the Innovera MTS Device safe for at-home use?
Yes. The device is designed with safety features including adjustable needle depth and auto-stop technology, suitable for all skin types.
How soon will I see results?
Many users notice improvements in skin texture and radiance within 2–4 weeks of consistent use. Individual results may vary.
Does it hurt?
Most users describe the sensation as a mild tingling. The adjustable intensity lets you find your comfort level.
Can I use my own serums with the device?
Absolutely. The MTS device enhances absorption of water-based serums. We recommend avoiding retinol or acids immediately after treatment.
What's included in the Starter Kit?
The kit includes the MTS device, replacement cartridges, hydrating prep serum, soothing cream, quick-start guide, and a premium carrying case.
